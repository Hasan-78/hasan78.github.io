Smashed Beef Burgers #1

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 4 to 8 people
Ingredients Quantity
Ground beef 1 1/2 pounds
Mayo 1/3 cup
Mustard 1 teaspoon
salt 1/8th teaspoon
Pepper 1/8th teaspoon
Buns 4-8
Cheese Slice 4-8

Step 1: Prepare Patties – Divide 1 1/2 lbs of ground beef into 8 equal portions (3 oz each), cover, and refrigerate until ready to cook.

Step 2:Make Sauce – Stir together 1/3 cup mayo and 1 tsp mustard and set aside.

Step 3:Prep Toppings – fully prep your toppings ahead to assemble the burgers right away while the patties are hot and juicy off the grill.

Step 4:Toast buns – lightly butter the cut side of your buns and toast on a griddle or skillet over medium heat until golden brown. Transfer to a rack.

Step 5:Cook patties – increase to medium-high heat. Depending on your surface area, place 2 to 4 cold pieces of meat onto the hot surface. Working quickly, cover each piece with a sheet of parchment paper and smash each into a thin patty. Remove parchment paper, season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder, add a dab of sauce, and flip. Sear for 2 minutes on the first side and about a minute on the second side.

Step 6:Add cheese – place a slice of cheese onto half of the patties and top with a second patty and remove from heat. Serve warm over toasted buns with toppings.

Source: Natashas Kitchen

Boiled Shrimps #2

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Shrimps 1 1/4 pounds
Filtered water 8 cups
Garlic salt 1 tablespoon
Ground black pepper 1/2 teaspoon
Bay leaves 1-2

Step 1: Depending on how you are serving your shrimp, you can leave the shells on or off. I peel and devein first and keep the shells for the shrimp boil.

Step 2: Place the shells from the shrimp in a large sauce pan along with 1 Tbsp garlic salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper, and bay leaf. Add 8 cups water and bring to a boil over med/high heat.

Step 3:Once water boils, add the peeled and deveined shrimp and simmer until pink, about 2-3 minutes depending on size of the shrimp. Drain and transfer cooked shrimp to a bowl of cold water to stop the cooking process and let cool.

Instant Baked Potatoes #3

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Large Russet Potato 4
Cold Water 1 cup

Step 1: Prepare Instant Pot – place a trivet in the bottom of a 6 quart instant pot. Add 1 cup cold water.

Step 2:Scrub Potatoes – thoroughly wash the potatoes.

Step 3:Pierce Potatoes – poke the potatoes all over with a fork and arrange on the rack.

Step 4:Pressure Cook – Cover with lid and set the valve to the 'sealing' position. Set to manual high pressure for 16 minutes.

Step 5:Natural Pressure Release – once the cooking time is up, let the pressure release naturally (usually about 15 minutes) or until the pressure indicator drops indicating there is no pressure behind it. Switch the valve to the venting position to release any remaining steam before carefully removing the lid.

Step 6:Serve Potatoes - Poke potatoes to test for doneness, then remove them to a plate using tongs.Cut potatoes in half and serve with your favorite toppings (sour cream, cheese, chives), or let them cool and use them for Potato Salad.

Source: Natashas Kitchen
